**Najdorf Esports is a new Overwatch organization in the Pacific region.** Our first move: acquiring the roster that competed as **Rankers** in OWCS Pacific Stage 1.

This isn't a rebrand of Rankers. Najdorf Esports is a separate organization, with its own ownership and identity, that's bringing the Rankers lineup over intact under a new banner.

The squad carries over as-is. The five players who took Stage 1 are still the five going into Stage 2, plus the same coaching staff — they now compete under the Najdorf Esports name. What's new is the org behind them, the visual identity, and a clearer way to talk about how we want them to play.

We named the org after a chess opening on purpose. The Najdorf Variation is the Sicilian Defence's sharpest sound reply to 1.e4. It looks quiet at the surface — Black plays a sleepy little pawn move to a6 and waits. Then the game explodes into some of the most analyzed attacking lines in chess history. Bobby Fischer loved it. Kasparov used it twice to beat Karpov for the world title. That's the version of the game we want our players showing up to: patient setup, sharp execution.

OWCS Pacific Stage 2's main event runs from **June 4** through July 9. Roster announcements, match schedules, and watch-party plans will go up here and on our social channels in the weeks ahead.

For partnerships, press, or anything else, reach us at [owner@najdorfesports.gg](mailto:owner@najdorfesports.gg).

GLHF.
